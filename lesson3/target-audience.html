Target Audience

The target audience for this website is the retirement community of Mesquite, NV. The site is produced by West Side Sunny, a local retirement community, and is specifically designed for members of that retirement community. The site can be utilized by other retirees in Mesquite as well. If a person would like information about West Side Sunny events, local events, healthcare, human services, local news, schools, and local businesses, they would be considered a target person.

Personas

John Paul

Demographics:

  • Age: 65
  • Family Status: Married. He has 2 children, but neither live close

Goals:

  • Wants to keep track of local events
  • Wants to stay current on local news

Environment:

John is comfortable navigating the internet on his iphone and on his desktop. He loves the retirement community West Side Sunny because it allows he and his wife to participate in many activities. He also likes staying current on the high school's sports news.

Quote: "When is the next community pickleball tournament?"

Scenarios:

  • "When is the next hot air baloon festival in Mesquite?
  • "What was the score of te last high school softball game?
  • "Who is sponsoring the upcoming car show?"
  • "How do my wife and I sign up for the upcoming pickleball tournament?"

Mary Smith

Demographics:

  • Age: 68
  • Family Status: Widowed. He has 3 children. She has a son in Mesquite

Goals:

  • Wants to stay current on local news
  • Wants to keep current on available healthcare in Mesquite
  • Wants to keep track of crafting activities available in the retirment community

Environment:

Mary is comfortable navigating specific websites on her mobile phone and on her desktop. Her son has showed her how to navigate West Side Sunny's website, so she is especially comfortable on this site. Mary likes to keep to visit with the other ladies in the retirement community and participates in the craft activities. Sometimes doctors are known to come and go in Mesquite, so she also likes to keep track of what providers are available to her.

Quote: "Is my optometrist still available to see?"

Scenarios:

  • "What crafting activity can I attend next with my friends?
  • "What are the major events occurring in Mesquite right now?
  • "Has Mesa View Hospital hired a new anesthesiologist?"
